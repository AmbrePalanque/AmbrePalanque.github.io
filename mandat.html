Mandat is a game we made in 10 student's team, during the "workshop" week. A few weeks before the French presidential election of 2017, intention to abstain rate was extremely high we looked to help people to understand, through this platform, the challenges of politics.

Mandate gives you great responsibilities. As President, you are faced with many decisions. Your goal is to maintain your relations with the people and the patronage and to keep Growth. Drag the card to the right to accept the proposal and left to refuse it.
This game was developed in Ruby On Rails with regard to the back-end, and for the front-end, in scss with Bootstrap.
