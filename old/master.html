Master Humain Computer Interaction

As part of my internship at the Université Toulouse III Paul Sabatier, I was responsible for the redesign of the website of the Master in HCI, as well as for the development of the administration software (php) of this website. I have also designed the communication plan for the degree. For that purpose, I have produced a report on the possible means of communication for the Master’s degree. Upon request of the head of the Master’s degree, I have presented the recommendations for selecting appropriate means of communication.

The previous version of the web site was not effective because of its indexed pages. Many were obsolete, and the same information was available in the various pages. Therefore, I have taken over the whole tree of the site, so that it is accessible more easily. During this first internship experience, I have gained skills in analyzing and understanding know the customer’s expectations. I have acquired the capacity to evaluate the necessity of iterating and reworking the design of a website. This experience also encouraged me in not hesitating to give my own opinion and to provide guidance to customers. The main colors of the former website of the Master in HCI were red and white. For the new version, I have proposed two shades of gray and yellow. The gray color let the users experience a technical and masculine look and feel. The yellow color aims at being attractive and at adding femininity to the look and feel.
