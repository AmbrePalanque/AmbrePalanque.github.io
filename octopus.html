During our web design courses we had to create a creative agency and make the model of the site. For the Home page we have chosen a background video. We went on a simple and sleek design by playing on various sizes and thicknesses of font. We also made a design on various formats.

There are two versions for the iPad, one for the day and the other for the night. Even if a black text on a white background remains more readable, it is also much more aggressive for the eyes. That is why we made two versions. The "Day" version is composed of bright colors, yellow and pink, on a white background. As for the "Night" version we chose darker colors, with gray text. The dark versions are wanted "elegant", while the more colorful is in a young, dynamic mind. We chose fonts without serif, like the Roboto, but with varying thicknesses, and the Dolcevita for its beveled A.
