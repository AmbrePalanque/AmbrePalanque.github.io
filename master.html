As part of my internship at the IHM Master in Toulouse, I was responsible for designing the training website, as well as an administration software developed in PHP. I also designed a communication plan for the training, and a presentation of the means of communication to the respposable of it.

The old site of the master was not effective on its indexed pages, many were obsolete, and the information on various pages could be grouped in one. So I have taken over the whole tree of the site, so that it is accessible more easily. This first internship experience allowed me to analyze and know the expectations of a client, to discover the necessity to rework the design of the website several times, but also to be able to give his own opinion, Refuse proposals. The master's website was originally red and white. I chose two shades of gray, which give a technical and masculine side. The aim of the yellow is to be attractive and to add femininity to design.
