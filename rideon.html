Return

This project took place at the beginning of my second year in mmi. We were a team of 5 students, two mmi, two "infocom" option "pub", and a student in the license pro mediaplanning. The goal of the project was to review the entire marketing strategy of a customer. In our case it was the company "Ride On Experience" which offered as a service a rental of one wheel, on Arcachon, but wishing to extend on bordeaux.

We had a week to propose a new strategy to our client. Clement and I first did a complete audit of the website. We then offered them a website design and their flyers to be more adapted to their target (20-50 years old, active, used to new technology). We have chosen an orange color for a more sporting mind, and have sought to give more dynamism to the website.
